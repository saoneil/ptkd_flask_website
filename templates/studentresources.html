Student Resources


Below is the student manual. It contains rules of the Dojang (training space), a basic introduction to Taekwon-Do, a written list of most fundamental techniques that are taught in class, descriptions of the rank system, historical significance of the patterns, some of the testing requirements and step-by-step descriptions of all color belt patterns. It should be used as a reference and assist with training at home.
