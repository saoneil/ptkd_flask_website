Schedule

Advanced students are welcome to attend lower-level classes. Students should not attend classes above their rank without prior approval from Sean. The classes will be taught according to the ranks described below, so it is important to attend the classes for your level as much as possible.




The classes are held at St. Andrew's Community Center, 3380 Barnstead Lane, Halifax. We use the multipurpose room at the back of the building when entering through the main doors. Note that Barnstead Lane is only accessible from Bayers Road outbound by car.



Class Fees

New Students will be allowed to try the classes before registering for a period of about 3 weeks. Once the program is determined to be a good fit and the student wishes to continue with the program, the below fees are applicable:

  • Option 1) $55.00/month (tax incl.) for any/all of the above classes.
  • Option 2) $45.00/month (tax incl.) for any/all of the above classes for 3 months or more, or for multiple family members.

You can contact Sean O'Neil at performance_taekwondo@hotmail.com if you wish to inquire further.



Uniforms, Sparring Gear and Club T-Shirts.

Club T-Shirts are available. They will be ordered in bulk and are approximately $20. If you wish to order one, please fill out this form and notify Sean. Club shirts can be worn to Friday's classes: T-Shirt Google Form Order Sheet.

Uniforms are not required for students during the trial period. There are various styles and types of uniforms, all varying in cost (beginner uniforms are approximately $40, depending on size). Sparring gear is also available for purchase, although not required outside of competition and varies in cost depending on the style and size (the most affordable set is approximately $150). Please contact Sean O'Neil at performance_taekwondo@gmail.com to place an order for any equipment.
